Commercial Pilot License (CPL)

Overview

The Commercial Pilot License (CPL) allows you to fly for compensation or hire, making it a crucial step toward a professional aviation career.

Requirements

To enroll, you must hold a PPL, have a minimum of 150 flight hours, and meet specific medical and proficiency requirements.

What You'll Learn

Training covers advanced flight maneuvers, cross-country flights, and professional airmanship skills.

Flight Hours

A minimum of 250 total flight hours is required to qualify for a CPL.

Career Opportunities

A CPL allows you to work as a paid pilot, opening doors to charter flying, flight instruction, and airline careers.
