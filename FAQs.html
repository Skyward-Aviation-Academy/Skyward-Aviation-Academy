Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans designed to suit a range of budgets. Please contact our admissions team for detailed information on financing options.

Do you provide dormitory or housing services?

Skyward Aviation Academy partners with local housing providers to assist students in finding affordable accommodation. While we do not operate our own dorms, we can offer recommendations and support in securing housing.

What is included in the program fees?

Our program fees generally include aircraft rental, instructor costs, flight training hours, and ground school training. Detailed cost breakdowns are available on each specific program page.

What are the prerequisites for enrollment?

Prerequisites vary by program. For example, a Private Pilot License (PPL) requires a minimum age of 17 and a valid medical certificate, while a Commercial Pilot License (CPL) requires a PPL plus additional flight hours. Please check the individual program pages for detailed requirements.

Where can I find detailed cost breakdown information?

We provide comprehensive cost breakdowns—including hourly rates and total estimated costs—on each program’s dedicated page. For further details, please visit the Programs section of our website.

Do you offer simulator training, and is it included in the program fees?

Simulator training is available for additional practice and is billed separately from the standard program fees. Please refer to the specific program pages for additional simulator training rates.
