Fleet & Simulators

Our Aircraft Fleet

Skyward Aviation Academy operates a modern and well-maintained fleet, providing a variety of aircraft for different levels of training. Each aircraft is equipped with advanced avionics and safety systems to ensure a high-quality learning experience.

Aircraft by Training Program

  • Private Pilot License (PPL): - Cessna 172 Skyhawk (x4) - Piper PA-28 Warrior (x3)
  • Instrument Flight Rules (IFR): - Cessna 172 with G1000 (x2) - Piper Archer (x2)
  • Multi-Engine Rating: - Piper PA-44 Seminole (x2) - Beechcraft Baron 55 (x1)
  • Commercial Pilot License (CPL): - Cessna 182 Skylane (x2) - Cirrus SR22 (x1)
  • Certified Flight Instructor (CFI): - Cessna 172 (x2) - Piper PA-28 (x2)

Advanced Flight Simulators

Our academy provides cutting-edge flight simulation technology that allows students to practice complex flight scenarios in a controlled environment. These simulators are FAA-approved and designed to enhance training efficiency.

Simulators by Training Program

  • Private Pilot License (PPL): - Redbird TD2 Basic Training Simulator (x2)
  • Instrument Flight Rules (IFR): - Redbird MCX Full-Motion Simulator (x2)
  • Multi-Engine Rating: - Frasca Beechcraft Baron Simulator (x1)
  • Commercial Pilot License (CPL): - ALSIM AL250 Advanced Simulator (x1)
  • Certified Flight Instructor (CFI): - Redbird FMX with Instructor Mode (x2)

Maintenance & Safety

All aircraft undergo regular maintenance and safety inspections to ensure optimal performance. Our dedicated team of certified aircraft mechanics ensures that each aircraft meets FAA safety standards.

Hands-on Training

Students at Skyward Aviation Academy receive both real-world flight experience and simulator training. This combination allows for skill development in a safe and controlled setting before transitioning to actual flight operations.
