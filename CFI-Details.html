Certified Flight Instructor (CFI)

Overview

Become a Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) and teach aspiring pilots the skills they need to succeed.

Requirements

A CPL is required to enroll in the CFI program, along with meeting specific knowledge and flight proficiency standards.

What You'll Learn

Training covers instructional techniques, flight demonstration skills, and how to effectively teach aviation concepts.

Career Opportunities

A CFI certificate allows you to work as a flight instructor, gain flight hours, and build experience for an airline career.
