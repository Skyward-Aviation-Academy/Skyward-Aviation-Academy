Multi-Engine Rating

Overview

The Multi-Engine Rating allows you to operate aircraft with more than one engine, enhancing your flying capabilities and career opportunities.

Requirements

A valid Private Pilot License (PPL) or Commercial Pilot License (CPL) is required to enroll in this program.

What You'll Learn

Training includes multi-engine aerodynamics, engine-out procedures, and advanced flight techniques for twin-engine aircraft.

Flight Hours

A minimum of 10-15 flight hours in a multi-engine aircraft is required to complete this rating.

Career Opportunities

Multi-engine certification is essential for pilots aiming for airline or corporate flying roles.
